The 2015 Government Partners community is made up of leaders from the eight governments participating in this year's Fellowship.

Who we are

This community of practice is a discussion forum for our current government partners participating in the fellowship program to share stories, exchange resources, and help each other (and future government partners) get the most out of the program. Membership is by approval only.

Resources for 2015 Government Partners

We're compiling a set of resources, guides and tools useful for government partners who are working with their fellowship teams to apply user-centered, iterative approaches to civic challenges.

Guides

  • How do I get the most out of my government's Fellowship?

    Use this guide if you are looking for information about the goals, phases, and logistics of the Fellowship program.

  • What are the goals of a good city website?

    Use this guide to learn about what goals a city website should have for government and residents and general principles.

  • When and how should I use open data?

    Learn what open data is and how it could be a useful tool for increasing transparency and improving government services.

  • How can I better engage residents in government's work?

    Learn about strategies and practical tactics to meaningfully engage your community and make your government more responsive.

Tools

  • Tool: City Analytics Dashboard

    Deploy our City Analytics Dashboard, a free open source tool that uses Google Analytics to help city staff better understand their website’s live traffic.
